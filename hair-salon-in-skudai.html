Frequently Asked Questions About hair salon

What are your operating hours at KSL City Mall and do I need an appointment?
We're open daily from 10:30 AM to 9:30 PM at our KSL City Mall outlet (3rd Floor). While we accept walk-ins, we strongly recommend making an appointment to avoid waiting times, especially during weekends and public holidays. You can book an appointment by calling us at +607-123-4567 or through our WhatsApp booking service. We appreciate if you could arrive 5-10 minutes before your scheduled appointment time.
How much does a basic haircut and color service cost?
Our basic haircut starts from RM38 for ladies and RM28 for men. For hair coloring, prices start from RM168 depending on hair length and thickness. This includes basic L'Oréal professional hair color. For specialty colors like ash grey or fashion colors, prices start from RM288. We provide exact quotes during consultation as prices vary based on hair length, condition, and desired result. All prices include washing and basic styling.
Do you offer Korean-style perming, and how long does it take?
Yes, we specialize in Korean-style perming! A typical Korean volume perm takes about 2-3 hours depending on hair length and thickness. Prices start from RM238 for shoulder-length hair. Our stylists are trained in Korea and use premium Korean products. We recommend booking a consultation first to discuss the best perm type for your hair texture and desired style. The perm typically lasts 4-6 months with proper care.
What hair treatment options do you have for damaged hair?
We offer several treatment options for damaged hair, including Kerastase deep conditioning (RM128), Olaplex treatment (RM168), and our signature REDS intensive repair treatment (RM188). Each treatment takes 45-60 minutes and includes a hair analysis, scalp massage, and treatment application. We recommend a consultation first so our stylists can assess your hair condition and recommend the most suitable treatment.
Do you provide any warranty or guarantee for your coloring services?
Yes, we offer a 7-day guarantee for all our color services. If you're not satisfied with the color result, return within 7 days and we'll adjust it free of charge. However, this guarantee only applies if you haven't washed your hair with non-color-safe products or exposed it to chlorine. We also provide detailed aftercare instructions and recommend specific products to maintain your color's vibrancy longer.
